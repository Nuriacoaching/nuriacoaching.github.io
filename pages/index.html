Hello, I am Nuria

Why I do
this work.

I know what it is like to love someone and still feel profoundly alone in the relationship. To want things to change but have no real way to get through to your partner. To feel stuck between staying and leaving, knowing neither feels right.

For years I watched this pattern play out — in my own life, in my friends' relationships, in the quiet exhaustion of people who seemed to have everything together on the outside. What I kept noticing was not a lack of love or effort. It was a lack of actual tools.

After 15 years in senior leadership across multiple countries, I found myself drawn to the deeper question of what makes human connection work and fall apart. I pursued VITA certification in somatic and intimacy coaching, trained in Terry Real's Relational Life Therapy, and built a practice around one thing: giving people the skills they were never taught.

My approach

Warm enough to feel safe.
Honest enough to actually help.

This is not about talking through problems until you feel temporarily better. It is about developing a different capacity. One session at a time.

I work with people who are not ready to give up but cannot keep living the way they are living. People who want clarity, not ultimatums.
